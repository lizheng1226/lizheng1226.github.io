Let's take a look at the Genre Distribution!

We can see that both PS4 and Xbox players like the Action games most!

Role-Playing games seem to be dominating on the PS4 platform while Sport, Racing, and Shooter Games are three unique games genres in Xbox platform that outcompete its competitor in proportion.

How about the rank of Game Makers?

Activision, Ubisoft, and Electronic Arts share the Top 3 positions respectively both in PS4 platform and Xbox platform in terms of the total global sales.

Top 10 Games!

It seems like players from both platforms love AAA games!

Both Grand Theft Auto V and Red Dead Redemption 2 come from Rockstar Games, even though it only ranks 6th place on the Top 10 Game Makers Breakdown.

Final Games Sellling Battle!
